Welcome to the GARB Eye Tracking Research Study. Before participating, please read and agree to the following:

Purpose: This extension tracks where you look while reading online articles to help us understand reading patterns and improve reading assistance tools.

What we collect:

  • Eye gaze coordinates while reading
  • Time spent on each line of text
  • URLs and titles of articles you read while the extension is active
  • Reading session timestamps

What we DON'T collect:

  • Passwords or personal information
  • Browsing history when extension is not activated
  • Any data from non-article pages

Your rights:

  • You can withdraw at any time by clicking "Revoke Consent"
  • You can export your data at any time
  • Your data will be anonymized using a participant ID
